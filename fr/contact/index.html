Contact Us

DSK International is easily accessible by car or train (Rokko Liner, Island Center Station), approximately 20 minutes from Sannomiya (Kobe's down-town area) and 35 minutes from Umeda (Osaka's down-town area).

Enrolment Enquiries

Please email mail@dskobe.org or call +81 (0)78-857-9777 to speak to one of our office staff about enrolments.

Please visit our Admissions section of the website for further details.

Open Events

Our Open Events provide a fun and unique opportunity to spend time with current students, parents, teachers and staff. We have a variety of open events happening at our school and attendance is free and bookings usually aren't required. Please see a list of our events here.

Open Days

Our school has two open days every year: one in March and one in November. This is one of the best opportunities to come and explore our school, meet the staff and get a sense for what life is like at DSKI. Details of the next open day will be available on the events page very soon. If you are interested in booking your place at the open day please contact us here:
