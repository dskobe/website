DSKI Summer Camp

Our summer camps are designed for students to be able to join us in the vacation for a day filled with activities they can enjoy, using English as the language of communication.

We have a range of different activities running which the children can choose from such as sports, outdoor games, music workshop and art and crafts!

Non DSKI students are welcome to apply.

JOIN US FOR A SUMMER OF POSSIBILITIES!

Sample Description

WEEK ONE - Dingle Dangle Puppets

Explore the world of puppetry, develop linguistic skills, write original scripts and stage a puppet show.
Field Trip : Muji+ Creative Studio

WEEK TWO - Movers and Shakers

Design circuits, code games, build and code a robot, design a robot maze and create other movable projects.
Field Trip : Kawasaki Good Times World

WEEK THREE - Work with Wood

Use tools safely to construct with wood, use design thinking to create practical pieces, integrate technology and circuitry into finished projects.
Field Trip : Takenaka Carpentry Tools Museum

WEEK FOUR - Out of the Box

Indulge in creative cardboard construction, build movable automation, understand how gears work and create a mechanical toy.
Field Trip : Arimata Toys and Automata Museum
